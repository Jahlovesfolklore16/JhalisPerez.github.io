These are my art artifacts!


  1. Kaleidoscope

    2. I chose the autumn leaves of October because of All Too Well TV. When the song came out after a decade of waiting for it the short film did a number on me. The way that Taylor encaptured every element of her song made me melt into tears. “ Autumn leaves falling like pieces into place” that's what the photos I chose reminded me of. I made this project in hopes that I’d get a masterpiece but instead I got a result that looked unrecognizable to my original photo. I had to duplicate the layer so much I was surprised when I actually finished.

  2. Mandala

    3. To make this project I used photoshop and edit transform to achieve this project. The shape I chose was a flower. I wanted to do wisteria a flower that falls from the branches of trees with its purple vibrant color. That's the kind of vibe I was going for because of the lyric “ I want to watch wisteria grow right over my feet. Cause I haven’t moved in years”- Taylor swift. That song is on the deluxe version of Folklore, it adds a great escapism plot to the story she tells on the album. Since I did it a little off center it's kind of dropping but if you let the song speak to you you would understand why it's a little droopy.

  3. Fantastical Beast

    4. The animal I chose is the best pet ever: a cat, more specifically a ragdoll. I made a demon, angel, and cat. The irony am I right. The cat can fly and make people happy but it comes at a price. In order to stay happy you need to listen to Taylor Swift for the rest of your life. I looked for pegasus wings and demon horns( Taylor nation is in the horns). I had to make layers and erase what wasn’t needed to create the masterpiece Cinnabon.

  4. What's Your Vibe

    5. I made this project during Taylor Swifts Midnight Mayhem with me era. She just revealed that track 2 in her new album Midnights is called Maroon hence the background. Mirrorball is one of the songs on Folklore I relate to the most because I've never been a natural at anything. I've always had to try, try, try. I put fenty on the project because I use classy lip gloss not some beauty supply lip gloss. Using express made this project easier and more entertaining.

  5. Pop Art Celebrity

    6. Rachel Chinouriri is upcoming Black artist that I think deserves more recognition. Her most famous song is 'So my darling' it was popular for a short time on tik tok but thats not how I discovered her. She had showed up on my for page promoting her new single 'All I ever asked'and so I presaved it on spotify. I started listening to more of her music and decided to use this project to prompt her music by using photoshop.
